My Projects


I am actually working on a data-mining project as team leader and developer in a 5 members team. The project is based on the Libon application from the Orange company and began in March 2015. The main purpose is to build a complete pipeline, from the detection of communities among a social network of Libon users with Spark-GraphX to data visualisation thanks to Zeppelin.

Scala Spark GraphX Zeppelin
