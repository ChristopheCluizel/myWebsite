Who Am I?

My name is Christophe Cluizel. I'm a 5th year student in an engineering school called INSA in France. I have studied machine learning, data mining and I'm impatient to be able to apply these concepts in the real data world!

I'm keen on computer science and I love learning and discovering new technologies, languages or other tools. Especially, data science and Scala are my cup of tea. I have already played with nice stuff like Spark, GraphX and Zeppelin to deal with big amount of data.
